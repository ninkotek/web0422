EVENT

White Castle, home of the iconic “slider” hamburger, broke ground today on the first Arizona restaurant for the 98-year-old family owned business. Located at 9310 East Via de Ventura in the Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian Community’s Talking Stick Entertainment District near Scottsdale, White Castle expects to start satisfying the taste buds of Arizona “Cravers” by Fall 2019. In attendance for the groundbreaking ceremony was legendary shock rocker and Cravers Hall of Fame member, Alice Cooper.
